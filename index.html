POWERFUL MAGNETIC FIELDS

The most powerful magnetic fields in the universe are from magnets - neutron stars with crazy magnetic fields. How crazy is that? On Earth, your typical magnet has a strength of about 1.25 Tesla. A magnetar will have 1,010 Tesla. This is so strong that the magnetic field would literally kill you if you get within 1,000 kilometers of the magnetar by distorting the electrons in your body too much. All the electrons in the atoms in your body would be stripped away, so all the molecules would break apart. So you'd be ionized into a whiff of plasma, basically.
